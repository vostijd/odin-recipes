French Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Splash of Milk

Steps

  1. Add eggs, vanilla and eggs to bowl
  2. Mix until solid consistency
  3. Soak both sides of bread in mixture
  4. Shake some cinnamon on it
  5. Cook in oven on 360 degrees for 15-20 minutes
